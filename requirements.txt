-----------------------------
--- Hardware Requirements ---
-----------------------------
--- Minimum Requirements (for basic functionality) ---

Processor: Intel Core i3 or AMD Ryzen 3 (or equivalent)
Memory (RAM): 8 GB
Graphics Card: Integrated Graphics (low FPS expected)
Webcam: Any standard internal or external webcam

--- Recommended Requirements (for smooth real-time performance) ---

Processor: Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 (or better)
Memory (RAM): 16 GB or more
Graphics Card: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3050 or better (for CUDA acceleration and high FPS)
Webcam: A high-definition (1080p) webcam for better input quality

-----------------------------
--- Software Requirements ---
-----------------------------

Operating System: Windows 10/11, macOS 11 (Big Sur) or newer, or a modern Linux distribution (e.g., Ubuntu 20.04+)
Python Version: 3.8 - 3.12
